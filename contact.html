Join Us!

Join us!

We are actively looking for students to join our group. Feel free to contact us if you are interested. People with basic experience in natural language processing and are desired to explore in this field will be preferred.
For undergraduate students you can apply to attend our weekly discussion panel or paper seminar every semester.

Graduates

If you are willing to apply for PhD/master positions in our group, please contact ybwu@cs.ecnu.edu.cn for further details. People with basic understranding of related fields, prior experience in natural language processing and proficient coding skills are preferred.

Undergraduates

Starting from 2017, in each year we hold a seminar (click here for more details) dedicated to junior students, in which we introduce fundamentals in natural language processing and latest advance in related fields. If you are currently in your junior year and are interested in working with us in the future, we highly recommend you to join our seminar. You can reach out to ybwu@cs.ecnu.edu.cn or weiyang@godweiyang for more details.
